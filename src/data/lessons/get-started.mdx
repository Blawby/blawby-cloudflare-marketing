# Getting Started with Blawby

Welcome to Blawby. This guide helps you set up the essentials so your firm can accept payments quickly, stay organized, and avoid compliance mistakes early.

## Quick Start Guide

Use the following steps to get started with Blawby:

1. **Sign Up for a Blawby Account.** (2 minutes) Visit [https://blawby.com](https://blawby.com) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and create a secure password. Provide your law firm's details and complete the verification process. For more information, see [Setting Up Payouts](/payments/payouts).
   - **Why this matters:** this creates your firm workspace and gives your team one secure place to manage client payments.
2. **Set Up Your Account.** (5 minutes) Connect your bank account by going to **External payout accounts** in your dashboard and following the prompts. Submit required business and representative information, including requested verification documents. Add accepted payment methods in **Settings > Payments** (card, ACH, or both). Ensure verification is complete to enable payouts. For a detailed guide, visit [Payouts](/payments/payouts).
   - **Why this matters:** without verification, payments may process but payouts can be delayed.
3. **Get Your Custom Payment Link.** (2 minutes) In your dashboard, go to **Settings** and select your Team name. Scroll to **Payment Link**, enter a unique link name (example: `myfirm-payments`), then save and copy your link. You can pre-fill amounts by adding `amount` in cents (for example: `https://blawby.com/your-link-name?amount=1000` for $10.00). For more details, see [Payments](/payments/accepting-payments).
   - **Why this matters:** a consistent payment link makes it easier for staff to request payment and for clients to pay quickly.
4. **Share Your Payment Link and Start Accepting Payments.** (2 minutes) Add your payment link to invoices, intake emails, website buttons, or direct client messages. Clients can pay securely by card or ACH, and receipts are sent automatically. Track payment activity in your dashboard under **Payments**. For more information, see [Payments](/payments/accepting-payments).
   - **Why this matters:** faster payment flow improves cash flow and reduces time spent chasing balances.

## Recommended Next Steps

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does it take to set up a Blawby account?

Most users can complete the full account setup, including verification, in under 15 minutes if all necessary documents are ready.

### Do I need a website to accept payments?

No. You can use your Blawby payment link directly in emails, invoices, or messages—no website needed.

### Can I customize the payment link?

Yes. You can create a custom link name and add default amounts. You can also modify the amount dynamically in the URL.

### Are payments secure?

Absolutely. All payment processing through Blawby is encrypted and compliant with industry standards.

### Will clients receive confirmation of their payment?

Yes. Both you and your client will receive email receipts immediately after a payment is completed.

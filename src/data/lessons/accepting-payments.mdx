## How to Accept a Payment Using Blawby

Use the following steps to create and share a payment link with a client.

1. **Log in to your Blawby dashboard.** (1 minute) Visit [Blawby](https://app.blawby.com) and log in with your account credentials.
2. **Go to payment link settings.** (1 minute) From the sidebar, select **Settings**, click your firm name, and open **Payment link**.
3. **Create a payment link.** (2 minutes) Enter your custom link name (e.g., "consultation" or "donation") and press save.
4. **Customize the amount (optional).** (1 minute) To change the amount directly in the URL, modify the amount parameter in cents. Example: `https://blawby.com/myfirm-payments?amount=7500` (for $75.00).
5. **Send the link to your client.** (1 minute) Copy the payment link and send it via email, SMS, or any client communication channel.
6. **Track payment status.** (1 minute) Monitor your dashboard and payment notifications.

## How Payment Links Work

### Fast, Frictionless Payments for Every Client

With Blawby, you can generate a payment link in seconds—no technical setup required. Whether you're onboarding a new client, collecting a retainer, or following up on an outstanding invoice, payment links make it easy for clients to pay you online, from any device.

For example, when a client calls to ask how to pay their retainer, you can send them a secure payment link by email or text. They pay instantly, and both of you receive a receipt—no delays, no confusion.

## Using Dynamic Payment Links

Dynamic payment links let your team change the amount directly in the URL, so you can request different amounts without creating new links each time.

### Adjusting the Payment Amount

To change the payment amount, simply modify the `amount` parameter in the URL. The amount is specified in cents, so for example, to request a payment of $75.00, you would use:

`https://blawby.com/myfirm-payments?amount=7500`

This keeps payment requests simple and consistent across email, text, and intake workflows.

## Built-In Compliance, Less Admin

Blawby is designed to support compliance and reduce friction for law offices of any size:

- **Automatic separation of earned and unearned funds:** Payments are routed to the correct accounts, so you don't have to worry about manual sorting or accidental commingling.
- **No fees deducted from trust:** Processing and platform fees are always billed to your operating account, never from client funds.
- **Clear, searchable audit trails:** Every transaction is logged and easy to review, making audits and reconciliations faster and less stressful.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Blawby compliant with ABA and IOLTA trust accounting rules?

Yes. Blawby is designed to ensure full compliance with both ABA and IOLTA trust accounting requirements. Payments are automatically split and routed to the correct accounts.

### Can clients pay with ACH and credit cards?

Yes, Blawby supports secure credit card and ACH bank transfer payments.

### Are fees deducted from client trust accounts?

No. Processing and platform fees are charged only to your operating account—never from client funds.

### How fast do payments process?

Most payments are processed within 1–2 business days.

### Can I customize the payment page?

Yes. Each payment page displays your firm's branding, including logo, name, and contact information.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- If your payment link isn't working, ensure your payout account and payment method are set up in your account settings.
- For urgent payments, use the pre-filled amount feature to make it easy for clients to pay the exact amount you request.
